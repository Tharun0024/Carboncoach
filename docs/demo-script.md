# CarbonCoach - Live Demo Script

**Duration:** 3 Minutes  
**Presenter Roles:** 1 Presenter showing the UI and explaining the workflow.

---

## 1. Intro: The "Eco-Anxiety" Problem (0:00 - 0:45)
- **Visual**: Show the [Landing Page](file:///y:/PW3/frontend/src/app/page.tsx).
- **Script**: 
  > *"Every carbon footprint tool ends with a number—usually a scary number in tonnes of carbon. You feel eco-anxiety, but you don't know what to do next. The advice is always generic, like 'drive less.' That's where CarbonCoach comes in. It is not just another calculator—it is an AI-powered habit coach."*

---

## 2. Onboarding: A Warm Conversation (0:45 - 1:45)
- **Visual**: Navigate to [Chat Page](file:///y:/PW3/frontend/src/app/chat/page.tsx). Type *"Hi!"*.
- **Script**: 
  > *"Instead of asking you to fill out 40 different spreadsheet fields, CarbonCoach learns about you through conversation. Our Claude-powered backend guides you category by category—asking about your commute, flights, energy, and diet."*
- **Action**: Enter sample inputs (e.g., *"I drive 30km daily, fly twice a year, and eat beef three times a week"*).
- **Script**:
  > *"Once the coach has gathered enough data, it extracts the details into structured JSON, calculates your footprint, and builds your custom profile."*

---

## 3. The Dashboard: Action over Awareness (1:45 - 2:45)
- **Visual**: Navigate to the [Dashboard](file:///y:/PW3/frontend/src/app/dashboard/page.tsx).
- **Script**: 
  > *"Here is your dashboard. We see our footprint breakdown—Transport is our biggest area of impact. But more importantly, look below: we are assigned exactly ONE high-impact action this week: 'Lower Your Thermostat by 1°C'. We focus on one habit at a time."*
- **Action**: Click the "I did it!" button. Show completion state.
- **Script**: 
  > *"When you click 'I did it!', your carbon savings are added to your timeline, and your streak count increases. If you skip, the AI coach asks for your excuse and adjusts future assignments to help you stay on track."*

---

## 4. Conclusion: Real Reduction (2:45 - 3:00)
- **Script**: 
  > *"By breaking down carbon footprints into weekly bite-sized habits, CarbonCoach converts awareness into actual carbon reductions. Thank you!"*
